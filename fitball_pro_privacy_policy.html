Wayuu Automations Ltd (“Wayuu”, “we”, “our” or “us”) provides the Fitball Pro mobile application (“App”) for coaches and players to manage football training and view team data. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1) What we collect

We collect the minimum data needed to run the App.

  • Account information. Email address and a unique user ID when you sign in (via Firebase Authentication). We do not store your password.
  • App content you create. Team/squad information, training entries, notes and other content you upload or create in the App. Stored in Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore/Storage).
  • Device & app metadata (limited). Basic technical data from our service providers (e.g., IP address at time of request, device/OS/app version, timestamps). We do not collect precise location, Health/HealthKit data, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera.

Not collected: advertising identifiers for tracking, third‑party ad data, or analytics profiles. A future “single‑device limit” feature may record a device identifier; we will update this policy before enabling it.

2) How we use your data

  • App functionality & account management (sign‑in, secure storage, syncing).
  • Support & safety (diagnosing problems, preventing abuse).
  • Service communications about critical changes.

We do not use your data for third‑party advertising or cross‑app tracking.

Legal bases (EEA/UK): performance of contract (to provide the App), legitimate interests (security, support), or consent where required.

3) Sharing

We don’t sell your data. We share it only with:

  • Service providers acting on our instructions to run the App (primarily Google Firebase/Google Cloud; Apple’s services for distribution; crash/diagnostic infrastructure if enabled).
  • Authorities when required by law.

4) International transfers

Data may be processed in your region and/or other countries (including the UK, EU, or USA) by our service providers. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses).

5) Retention

Account and app content are retained while your account is active. You can request deletion at any time (see Your rights). We may retain limited records as required by law or for dispute resolution.

6) Security

Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and access is restricted. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we apply reasonable technical and organizational measures.

7) Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, export, or object to certain processing of your data.

  • Delete account/data: email us from your registered address and we’ll delete your Firebase Auth account and associated records, normally within 30 days.
  • Export: request a copy of your data via email.

8) Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). Organizations using the App are responsible for obtaining any necessary parental permissions where applicable.

9) Changes

We’ll update this Policy as the App evolves. We’ll post the new version with a new “Last updated” date and, when required, notify you in‑app or by email.
